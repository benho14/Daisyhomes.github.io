History

Daisy Homes

Founded in 2007,in memory of the family's late, grandmother Daisy.

Daisy Homes was founded by Iona Devendran, inspired in memory of the family's late, grandmother Daisy, where She lived in an aged care facility overseas where she received a very poor standard of care, that even included physical abuse.

This is why Daisy Homes so heartily embrace Core Values that include the rights of residents to :

- Respect, dignity and privacy

- A safe and homelike environment

- High quality care appropriate to a person's need

- Have an effective voice in matters that concern residents

- Social independence and freedom of choice, to the extent it does not unreasonably infringe on the rights of others

- Today Daisy Homes is currently owned and managed by Ivan Devendran and Shami Ekanayake.
